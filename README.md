# EchoAI

Ladies and gentlemen, meet EchoAI—your personal reflection engine. EchoAI listens to your words and thoughtfully returns them, helping you verify intent, test prompts, and refine your ideas in real time. It’s built on the robust OpenAI platform, so every repeat is crisp, context-aware, and exactly as you spoke it. Whether you’re debugging a prompt, rehearsing a presentation, or simply exploring AI interaction, EchoAI is your steadfast conversational mirror.
